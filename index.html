Bank Marketing EDA¶

Objectives¶

In this project, we would like to look at the distribution of customer age from the bank marketing data, which we downloaded from the UCI Machine Learning Repository.

Pseudocode¶

In [ ]:
import all the required modules

read csv and save as dataframe

drop all the rows with na values

plot histogram of age
